A Story Written in the Stars

Some love stories are not written by hands.
They are woven into constellations,
whispered between galaxies,
sealed in the quiet light of a July sky.
Before you found each other,
the stars already knew your names.
They had been waiting,
patient as the universe itself,
for the night two souls
finally recognised what they were to each other.
Twenty-seven years of mornings.
Twenty-seven years of home.
And still, when you hold hands,
the cosmos holds its breath.
This is your story.
It was always written in the stars.
